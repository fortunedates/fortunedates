About Us

Fortune Dates is a leading exporter/importer specializing in a wide variety of dates sourced directly from the rich lands of Saudi Arabia. With a commitment to excellence and quality, we bring you the best dates to tantalize your taste buds and enrich your culinary experiences.

Our Products

Medjool Dates

Known as the "King of Dates", Medjool dates are prized for their large size, soft texture, and rich caramel-like flavor.

Deglet Noor Dates

Deglet Noor dates are semi-dry with a slightly chewy texture and a sweet, nutty flavor profile. They are versatile and perfect for both snacking and cooking.

Khudri Dates

Khudri dates are characterized by their dark brown color and firm texture. They have a sweet taste with hints of caramel and are often enjoyed as a healthy snack.

Ajwa Dates

Ajwa dates are dark brown and have a soft, luscious texture. They are renowned for their rich flavor and are highly esteemed for their health benefits.

Sukkari Dates

Sukkari dates are light golden-brown and have a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture. They are exceptionally sweet with a honey-like taste.

Barhi Dates

Barhi dates are small and round with a soft, creamy texture. They have a rich, caramel-like flavor and are often enjoyed fresh or in desserts.
